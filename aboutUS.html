About Us

Feedables is a global community that connects our work together to fight against hunger and food wastage to create a brighter future.

Founded in 2024, Feedables has grown to become one of the most effective platforms and wide-reaching environmental organizations globally. Thanks to the contribution and effort of over millions of people around the world, we have made a significant impact on reducing food waste and helping those in need.

OUR MISSION

We are a social enterprise committed to combating food insecurity through innovative technology. Our web platform connects those in need with solutions, harnesses crowd-sourced ideas to fight hunger, and uses data to make potent decisions. We aim to raise awareness, improve lives, and foster connections locally and globally.

OUR VISION

A world where technology helps to bridge the gap between surplus and scarcity of food in hopes that no one will ever have to worry about hunger.
